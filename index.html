OUR TECHNOLOGY

MECHANICAL MASTERY

PRECISION ENGINEERING
Our mechanical watches feature intricate gear systems with mainsprings powering precise movements. The balance wheel and escapement mechanism regulate timekeeping to within seconds per day. Each component is hand-finished by our master watchmakers for optimal performance and longevity.

QUARTZ PRECISION

BATTERY-POWERED ACCURACY
Our quartz movements utilize the piezoelectric properties of quartz crystals to maintain exceptional accuracy. When electrified, the crystal vibrates at 32,768 Hz, providing a stable frequency that our integrated circuits convert into precise one-second pulses for the motor that moves the watch hands.

SMART TECHNOLOGY

CONNECTED INNOVATION
Our smartwatches combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology, featuring AMOLED displays, multi-core processors, and advanced sensors. With Bluetooth 5.0, NFC, and WiFi connectivity, these watches seamlessly integrate with your digital life while maintaining Alberto's signature style.

OUR PRODUCTS

LUXURY WATCHES

TIMELESS ELEGANCE
Our luxury watches represent the pinnacle of horological craftsmanship. Each timepiece is assembled by hand using premium materials like 18k gold, platinum, and sapphire crystals. Featuring intricate mechanical movements with complications like perpetual calendars, moon phases, and tourbillons, these watches are heirlooms meant to last generations.

VINTAGE WATCHES

HISTORY REDISCOVERED
Our vintage collection features carefully restored timepieces from the 1920s through the 1980s. Each watch is authenticated, serviced, and restored by our master watchmakers using period-correct parts when available. These mechanical marvels offer a tangible connection to horological history with their unique patinas and classic designs.

SMART WATCHES

MODERN CONVENIENCE
Our smartwatches blend cutting-edge technology with Alberto's design aesthetic. Featuring health monitoring (ECG, SpO2, sleep tracking), GPS, and multi-day battery life, these watches keep you connected without sacrificing style. Customizable watch faces and interchangeable straps allow personalization to match any occasion.

CLOCKS

HOME & OFFICE TIMEPIECES
From elegant mantel clocks to precision atomic wall clocks, our collection ensures accurate timekeeping with style. Featuring quartz, mechanical, and radio-controlled movements, these clocks are crafted from fine woods, metals, and glass. Many feature Westminster chimes, silent movements, or temperature/humidity displays for added functionality.

CUSTOMER SUPPORT

REPAIR SERVICES

EXPERT WATCHMAKING
Our certified watchmakers provide comprehensive repair services for all mechanical and quartz watches. From simple battery replacements to complete movement overhauls, we use specialized tools and genuine parts to restore your timepiece to factory specifications. All repairs come with a 1-year warranty on workmanship.

AUTHENTICATION

GENUINE CERTIFICATION
Our authentication service provides documented verification of your timepiece's authenticity. Using magnification, movement analysis, and comparison with manufacturer databases, we can confirm the legitimacy of your watch or clock. Receive a detailed certificate suitable for insurance purposes or resale documentation.

CUSTOMER CARE

DEDICATED SUPPORT
Our customer care team is available via phone, email, or live chat to answer questions about your Alberto timepiece. We provide guidance on care, operation, and troubleshooting. For complex issues, we can arrange in-store consultations with our watchmakers. All Alberto watches come with a 5-year international warranty.
